# SEO Strategy: 100% Organic Traffic Playbook

## The Core Insight

Angus doesn't compete for "PDF converter" (billions of results).
He owns "bank statement to excel" (niche, high-intent, professional).

---

## Keyword Strategy

### Tier 1: Money Keywords (High Intent)
```
┌────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  These are the keywords that directly lead to conversions      │
├────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│  • "convert bank statement pdf to excel"                       │
│  • "bank statement converter"                                  │
│  • "pdf bank statement to spreadsheet"                         │
│  • "[BANK NAME] statement to excel" (Chase, Wells Fargo, etc)  │
│  • "import bank statement into excel"                          │
└────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Tier 2: Problem-Aware Keywords
```
• "how to convert bank statement to excel"
• "extract transactions from bank statement"
• "bank statement data extraction"
• "turn bank statement into spreadsheet"
```

### Tier 3: Long-Tail Gold
```
• "chase bank statement to excel converter"
• "wells fargo pdf statement to csv"
• "barclays statement converter online"
• "extract data from [BANK] pdf statement"
```

**Why long-tail wins:**
- Less competition
- Higher intent (they know exactly what they want)
- Bank-specific pages = hundreds of ranking opportunities

## Keyword Research Template

Use this to find your own niche keywords:

```
[ACTION] + [FILE TYPE] + [SPECIFIC CONTEXT]

Examples:
- merge + pdf + invoices
- convert + image + to passport photo
- compress + pdf + for email attachment
- extract + text + from scanned document
- remove + background + product photo
```

## Quick Win: Google Search Console Mining

1. Go to Search Console → Performance
2. Filter by pages (your tool pages)
3. Look at queries you rank #5-20 for
4. Create dedicated pages for those queries
5. Watch rankings climb

This is free, uses your existing data, and often reveals opportunities you didn't know existed.
