## Writing Guidelines

### Content Structure (Answer-First Format)
```
1. TL;DR Answer (40-60 words)
2. Key Benefits (3-4 bullet points)
3. Step-by-step instructions with images
4. Common problems & solutions
5. Comparison with alternatives
6. FAQ section (5-7 questions)
```

### Technical Specifications
- **Word count**: 1,200-1,800 words
- **Reading level**: 8th grade (Hemingway Editor target)
- **Images**: Every 200-300 words + screenshots
- **Code blocks**: Syntax highlighted, copyable
- **Internal links**: 3-5 links to other tools/blog posts

### SEO Requirements
- **Title**: 50-60 characters, include primary keyword
- **Meta description**: 150-160 characters, benefit-focused
- **URL slug**: Short, descriptive, include keyword
- **H1**: Direct answer to main query
- **H2s**: Questions users actually ask
- **Schema**: HowTo, FAQPage, Article markup
